Refund & Return Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction and hassle-free returns

Introduction

At Interiors Nova, we are committed to your complete satisfaction. Since our business model involves taking an advance payment to begin work on your furniture, we have a specialized refund and cancellation policy. Please read this policy carefully to understand your rights and our procedures regarding advance payments, custom orders, and returns.

1. Advance Payment & Cancellation Policy

We require an advance payment (deposit) to confirm your order and begin the manufacturing and customization process.

Cancellation Before Work Begins

  • If you cancel before we begin work, 90% of your advance will be refunded within 7 business days
  • 10% will be retained as a cancellation processing fee
  • Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing via email

Cancellation After Work Has Started

  • If you cancel after we have begun work, the advance payment is non-refundable
  • You will be responsible for 100% of the advance amount and any materials already procured
  • If significant work has been completed, additional charges may apply

Refund Timeline

  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Bank processing may take an additional 3-5 business days

2. Return Eligibility (Post-Delivery)

To be eligible for a return of completed custom furniture, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • The item must be reported as defective or not as specified
  • Return request must be made within 30 days of delivery
  • The furniture must not have significant damage due to customer mishandling
  • Original documentation must be provided

Note: Custom furniture is generally non-returnable once completed and delivered, except in cases of manufacturing defects or items not matching agreed specifications.

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned under this policy:

  • Custom or personalized furniture once manufacturing has begun
  • Items showing signs of use, damage, or customer mishandling
  • Delivered furniture accepted by customer without immediate dispute
  • Items purchased more than 30 days ago
  • Partially completed or partially used items

4. Damaged or Defective Items Upon Delivery

If your furniture arrives damaged or defective, we want to make it right immediately:

  • Report damage within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage and packaging
  • Contact our customer service team with your order number
  • Do not discard the damaged item or packaging
  • We will arrange for a replacement or repair at no cost to you
  • Return shipping for damaged items will be covered by us

5. How to Initiate a Return

Follow these steps to initiate a return:

  • Step 1: Contact our customer service team at interiorsnova.store@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return
  • Step 2: Receive return authorization and return shipping address
  • Step 3: Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  • Step 4: Ship the item to the address provided (customer pays return shipping unless item is defective)
  • Step 5: We will inspect the item upon receipt
  • Step 6: Upon approval, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

6. Return Shipping

Prepaid Return Shipping: We cover return shipping costs for defective, damaged, or incorrect items.

Customer-Paid Return Shipping: For items being returned due to customer preference or change of mind (within the 30-day return window), the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. Customers should use a reputable courier service and retain tracking information.

Non-Returnable Items: Items outside the 30-day return window or that do not meet return eligibility criteria cannot be returned, and return shipping fees will not be reimbursed.

7. Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:

  • Inspection typically takes 2-3 business days
  • If approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Bank processing may take an additional 3-5 business days
  • Return shipping costs (when customer-paid) will not be refunded
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Any applicable taxes will be refunded if permitted by law

8. Defective or Incorrect Items

If you receive a defective, incorrect, or incomplete item:

  • Contact us immediately with photos and details
  • We will verify the issue within 24 hours
  • If confirmed, we will offer a replacement or full refund
  • Return shipping will be covered by us
  • Replacement items will be shipped at no additional cost
  • For critical defects, we may expedite shipping

9. Warranty Information

Our furniture comes with the following warranty coverage:

  • Manufacturing Defects: 1-year warranty against defects in materials and workmanship
  • Structural Issues: Covered under warranty if due to manufacturing defects, not customer use
  • Warranty Exclusions: Normal wear and tear, customer damage, misuse, and improper maintenance are not covered
  • Warranty Claims: Report warranty issues within 30 days of discovery with supporting evidence
  • Warranty Resolution: Repairs, replacement parts, or full replacement at our discretion

10. Exceptions to Return Policy

The following situations are exceptions to our standard return policy:

  • Custom Orders: Non-returnable unless defective or incorrect
  • Final Sale Items: Non-returnable as marked at time of purchase
  • Clearance Items: Non-returnable unless defective
  • Items Assembled: Cannot be returned unless damaged upon delivery
  • Special Orders: Non-returnable unless made in error by Interiors Nova

11. Customer Responsibilities

To ensure a smooth return process, please:

  • Keep all original packaging and materials
  • Handle items with care to maintain condition
  • Provide accurate contact information
  • Keep tracking information for returned items
  • Respond promptly to communications from our support team
  • Provide clear photos of any damage or defects
